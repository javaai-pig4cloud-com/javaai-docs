# Observability

Spring AI provides comprehensive observability features to help you monitor and debug your AI applications.

## Overview

This section covers the observability features available in Spring AI, including:

- Metrics collection
- Tracing
- Logging
- Health checks

## Getting Started

To enable observability in your Spring AI application, you need to add the appropriate dependencies and configuration.

## Features

### Metrics

Spring AI integrates with Micrometer to provide detailed metrics about your AI model interactions.

### Tracing

Distributed tracing support is available through Spring Cloud Sleuth and OpenTelemetry.

### Logging

Structured logging is supported out of the box with detailed information about model interactions.

### Health Checks

Health indicators are provided to monitor the status of your AI model connections.
